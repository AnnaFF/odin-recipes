Bibimbap



Description

Bibimbap (비빔밥), which literally means "mixed rice," is a popular and visually vibrant Korean dish served as a hearty one-bowl meal. It is typically composed of warm white rice topped with an assortment of seasoned vegetables, a protein (commonly marinated beef), and an egg.

Ingredients

  • Marinated beef
  • White rice
  • Cucumber
  • Kimchi
  • Fried egg
  • Bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Prepare the vegetables
  4. Prepare the beef
  5. Put everything in a bowl
