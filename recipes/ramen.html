Ramen



Description

A typical ramen recipe creates a Japanese noodle soup featuring a rich, flavorful broth, chewy alkaline noodles, and various toppings. The essence of the dish lies in the harmony of its five core components: the broth, the tare (seasoning sauce), the noodles, the aroma oil, and the toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pork broth
  • Noodles
  • Bock choy
  • Boiled egg
  • Spring onions
  • Bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Simmer Broth
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Assemble in bowl
  4. Add toppings
